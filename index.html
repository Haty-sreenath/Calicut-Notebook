Our Story

Born in Malabar,
Beloved Worldwide

Pioneering in the world of fusion foods, Calicut Notebook had a humble start in 2012. From the very beginning, our vision has been to be the epitome of South Indian fusion food restaurants — offering authentic Kerala delicacies alongside inspired dishes from across the world, all at reasonable prices.

Our culinary team worked tirelessly to blend the best of French, Thai, Japanese, Arabic, and Indian cuisine with the essence of traditional Kerala flavours — creating signature dishes that Calicut Notebook is now known for, while staying true to the roots of timeless recipes.

"The much-praised, effortless yet impeccable service complements our best-in-class culinary expertise. Guests often describe our restaurants as their home away from home."

— Calicut Notebook, Est. 2012

Why We're Different

A Dining Experience
Like No Other

From the moment you step into our warmly lit dining room at Forum Center Edappal, the fragrance of Malabar spices and the gentle sounds of a kitchen alive with care envelop you. This is not just a meal — it's a passage into Kerala's richest culinary heritage.

Malabar Spice Heritage

Every dish is rooted in the ancient spice traditions of North Kerala — cinnamon, cardamom, kanthari, kodampuli, all sourced with care.

Global Fusion Inspiration

French, Thai, Japanese, Arabic and Indian techniques weave seamlessly with Kerala's kitchen to create truly one-of-a-kind dishes.

Free Home Delivery
